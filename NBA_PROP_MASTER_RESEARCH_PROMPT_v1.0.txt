OUTPUT 4 - LATE-SEASON APRIL 4, 2026 OVERLAY RULES
================================================================================

RULE 1 - SEEDING INCENTIVE TIERS

HIGH_URGENCY
- Within 2 games of a meaningful seed target or cutoff
- No clinch of desired position
- Five or fewer games remaining or every remaining game materially matters

MODERATE_URGENCY
- Meaningful seed movement still available
- Not immediate elimination or clinch pressure

LOW_URGENCY
- Team has something to play for, but marginal urgency is limited

LOCKED_OUT_RISK
- Seed effectively locked or improvement path functionally gone

ELIMINATED_RISK
- Team eliminated or operating like an eliminated team

RULE 2 - PLAY-IN INCENTIVE VS PLAY-IN ELIMINATION

- Play-in pursuit raises urgency for veteran minutes and closing continuity.
- Play-in elimination risk increases volatility because every loss changes team
  behavior sharply.

RULE 3 - HOME-COURT FIGHT VS LOCKED-SEED REST

- Home-court race supports stable veteran usage.
- Locked-seed teams carry elevated rest and soft-shutdown risk.

RULE 4 - SOFT SHUTDOWN / VETERAN REST RISK

Increase shutdown risk when:
- team is LOCKED_OUT_RISK or ELIMINATED_RISK
- player is older veteran
- player has heavy recent minute load
- player has prior load-management history

RULE 5 - ROTATION TIGHTENING VS EXPERIMENTAL LINEUPS

- HIGH_URGENCY teams usually tighten to trustworthy rotations.
- ELIMINATED_RISK teams often expand youth minutes and experimental lineups.

RULE 6 - YOUTH-MINUTE EXPANSION RISK

Raise risk when:
- team is eliminated
- fringe younger players have gained minutes in last 3 to 5 games
- veterans show reduced Q4 or blowout exposure

RULE 7 - FULL-SEASON DATA DISCOUNT

- Full-season averages are background only in April.
- Recent role-era data overrides old-season role context.

RULE 8 - WHEN L10 > L5 > L3

Prefer broader recent windows when:
- role is stable
- starting lineup is stable
- no recent injuries changed usage
- team motivation has not shifted materially

RULE 9 - WHEN TO INVERT AND PRIORITIZE L3

Prioritize L3 most heavily when:
- role changed recently
- teammate absence changed usage
- lineup configuration changed
- late-season urgency altered rotation behavior

RULE 10 - WHEN RECENT GAMES MISLEAD

Recent games can mislead when:
- they include blowouts
- they include foul-trouble or restriction games
- lineup configuration changed mid-window
- youth-minute expansion has started

RULE 11 - WHEN MOTIVATION CHANGES A BET VS WHEN IT IS NOISE

Motivation is material only when it plausibly changes:
- minutes
- lineup stability
- closing role
- veteran rest risk
- usage concentration

If it does not change one of those, it is background noise and must not be used
as narrative filler.
